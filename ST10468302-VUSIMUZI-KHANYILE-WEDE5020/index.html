About Us

GET TO KNOW TRINITY TECH SOLUTIONS

Trinity Tech Solutions was founded on 3 February 2023 by Vusimuzi Khanyile as a small, home-based IT support service. The company has since evolved to offer custom PC builds and web/software development, targeting individuals and small businesses. It is now recognized for providing dependable, affordable tech solutions.
